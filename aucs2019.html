AUCS Hackathon 2019

Improving interactions within local communities through tech

Team: Sunny Feng, Matteas Eden, Kimberley Evans-Parker, Josh Hill, Kelvin Ngor

Our Idea: Eventigate; a website to connect groups, specifically volunteering groups with schools and libraries. Individuals are able to look for locations which they would like to volunteer at and host events. While locations are able to find volunteer groups to host events at their location.

Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JS
